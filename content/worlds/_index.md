---
title: "Worlds"
description: "The settings of De Novo — interconnected worlds sharing a cosmology, a history, and an enemy."
---

De Novo is a shared cosmology spanning multiple worlds and eras. The settings are connected through the Terrans — an ancient spacefaring civilization waging a slow war against the Dark Tapestry — and through the consequences of that war reaching places that never asked to be involved.

Two worlds have seen active play: **Wiege**, a planet of nations and gods caught between local conflicts and cosmic forces they barely understand, and **The Shallow Sea**, a shattered mega-archipelago bearing the scars of a Great Old One's death.

Articles are tagged by knowledge scope:

- **Common** — what most people in the world would know
- **Uncommon** — what scholars, specialists, or the well-traveled would know
- **Player** — what has been revealed through campaign play and may contain spoilers
