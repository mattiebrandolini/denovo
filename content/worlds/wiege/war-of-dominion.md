The War of Dominion was a military campaign prosecuted by the Kingdom of [Kex]({{< ref "/worlds/wiege/kex" >}}) approximately fifty years before the present day of the 4th Era. Its primary target was [Noureddine]({{< ref "/worlds/wiege/noureddine" >}}), the holy city of Ronahiya, goddess of the sun.

The war culminated in the Ritual of Sundered Day — a mass ritual performed by Kex forces during the siege that killed a substantial number of combatants on both sides and resurrected them as undead. The ritual permanently cursed the city, transforming it into a megadungeon inhabited by two factions of undead who continue fighting each other decades later.

Kex understood the desecration as a theological victory: the subordination of a rival divine order to [Malabolous]({{< ref "/worlds/wiege/malabolous" >}})'s hierarchy. The [Coronal Jebrot]({{< ref "/worlds/wiege/the-coronal-jebrot" >}}), supreme leader of Ronahiya's faith, was lost during the war — her connection to her goddess was so total that she passed into Ronahiya's divine afterlife rather than undeath.

The consequences of the war persist. The ruins of Noureddine cast a shadow over the surrounding settlement. The undead within retain fragments of their former identities and their former hatred. And the living witnesses — those few who survived and remember — carry the weight of what was done and what was lost.
